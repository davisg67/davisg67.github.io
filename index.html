ABOUT ME

I have been a programmer since the late '90s where I got my first opportunity as a programmer working on lab calibration software that automated compression and tensile testing equipment, recorded and analyzed test data with graphing. During this time I was going to school and working towards two AS degrees for Network Management and Computer Programming. I had planned on being a network administrator, but I discovered I had a passion for programming as well, more than working with hardware.

In 2004 I started working as a freelancer providing network, server and desktop support as well as business software development services with VB6 and Microsoft Access Database. From here I moved on to developing with Alpha5 software, a rapid application development platform and relational database management system similar to FoxPro. During these years I saw that development was moving more from the desktop to the Web and I took the time to return to school to learn the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript web development. I then developed for a client a successful web-based medical claims management application using Alpha5 and my new front-end skills. This sounds much easier and smoother than it was, but I learned a great deal in this project and the client was happy.

By 2015 I started working as a fullstack WINS (Windows Server, IIS, .NET and MS SQL) web developer, working with VB.Net/Web Forms development. For three years I worked as a sole developer on creating a business intranet portal application to automate business tasks and combine data interfaces with multiple departments within the company. More recently I have started working with C# and learning MVC architecture as well as Linq and Entity framework. I'm also working to fill knowledge gaps in JavaScript and get a framework under my belt and eventually become a full-stack JS developer.

My main passion is programming, I see myself primarily as a coder more than anything else. When I'm not working I enjoy PC gaming (sorry console players), reading and working on my own projects or continuing education. I enjoy anything Star Wars, Marvel or fantasy/Sci-Fi in general. I also have a really sick fascination for really bad movies and sharknados.
